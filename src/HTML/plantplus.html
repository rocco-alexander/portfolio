Plant Plus Ltd.

Redesign and development of Irish plant hire business webpage.

Overview

Plant Plus Ltd. Is an Irish plant hire company that carries a wide selection of equipment for the construction industry. My role for this project was to update, redesign and develop their business page.

Role

Web Designer & Web Developer

About the Problem

Plant Plus had an outdated business presence. Task was to modernize the design of their business presence, while keeping much of the original content from the previous design.

Ideation

I took inspiration from other, construction websites and decided to go with a design that had a modern feel to it, while keeping subtle industrial notes in the color and font.I decided to go with a single page scroller website as opposed to the previous multipage design.

Final Product

The final product contains much of the previous design elements planned during the ideation phase. The typeface is sans-serif, yet bold. This gives the modern feel I was going for while adding a brawny element to the design which I believe is necessary for construction and industrial businesses.
